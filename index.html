It was a dark and stormy night...

In SALEM MASSACHUSETTS

the cod were scared, very scared.

Because the Proletariat were plotting their revenge

the revolutionary council were keen to act as the vanguard that

seize the means of butter production and establish the puritan gulag

but couldn't because of the general malaise that had eeked into every facet of their everyday lives.

they started to feel despondent and alienated inspite of the constant bellows of the town crier

and the new town board where anyone could post witty quips and gossip about who was predestined for heaven.
